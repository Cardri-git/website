Data Deletion Request Process

Dear Cardri User,

At Cardri, we prioritize the privacy and security of your data. If, for any reason, you wish to delete some or all of your data from Cardri, please follow the simple steps outlined below:

Data Deletion Request Process:

  • Login to Your Cardri App:
    • Open the Cardri App on your device.
    • Enter your login credentials to access your account.
  • Access Bottom-Right Menu:
    • Once logged in, navigate to the bottom-right menu on the home page.
  • Go to Settings:
    • From the menu, tap on "Settings" to access your account settings.
  • Scroll Down:
    • Scroll down on the Settings page to explore the available options.
  • Initiate Account Deletion:
    • Locate and tap on "Delete Account."

 

By following these steps, you can initiate the process to delete some or all of your data from Cardri. Your account and associated data will be permanently deleted upon completion of this process.

We appreciate your understanding and cooperation in this matter. If you have any further questions or concerns, please do not hesitate to reach out to our support team.

Thank you for choosing Cardri.

Best regards,

Cardri Team
